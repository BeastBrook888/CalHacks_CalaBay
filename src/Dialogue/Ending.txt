Congratulations on completing our game! We hope you had a fun and educational experience.
As you have journeyed through the game, you have learned the importance of protecting our planet and the environment. 
We believe that small actions can make a big difference and we encourage you to take what you have learned and apply it in your daily lives.
Remember to reduce, reuse, and recycle whenever possible. Plant trees, turn off lights when not in use, and conserve water. 
These simple steps can have a significant impact on our environment and make the world a better place to live in.
